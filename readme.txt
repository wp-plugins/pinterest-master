Pinterest Master adds the follow me on pinterest, pin it button, show pinterest profile and show pinterest board to your wordpress website.


== Description ==

Another great social network integrated with wordpress with the usual high quality of TechGasp, light, NO JAVASCRIPT OR AJAX for fast loading times and error free website.

Pinterest is a great booming social network that lets you organize and share all the beautiful things you find on the web, people use pinboards to plan their events like weddings, decorate their homes, organize their favorite recipies, etc. Pinterest Master adds the follow me on pinterest button, pin it button, show pinterest profile and show pinterest board to your website making easier for users and visitors to share "pin it" the page they are reading or follow your pinterest profile.

We made sure the pinterest buttons, profile or board display integrate perfectly in any template widget. Bring new visits and users to your wordpress powered website by pin it's pages or by showing off your photos. Also check Social Master for all social networks share buttons.

**pin it for wordpress...**

**LITE Version Contains:**

* Display or hide Widget Title
* Pinterest Follow Me Button

**ADVANCED Version Contains:**

[Advanced Version Link](http://wordpress.techgasp.com/pinterest-master/)

* Pinterest Follow Me Button
* Pinterest Pin It Button
* Display / Show Pinterest Board
* Display / Show Pinterest Profile
* Display or hide Widget Title
* Shortcode framework. Insert the widget inside pages and posts.

== Installation ==

1. Upload everything into the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory of your WordPress site.
2. Activate in the "Plugins" admin panel.
3. Visit the "Widget Page | Pinterest Master" to configure the settings and your template widget position.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Issues with buttons display =
* Please update to latest version. Up-to-date API's are available

= WHERE IS THE SHOW PINTEREST BOARD? =
* ADVANCED Version constains all Pinterest Buttons plus the Display Pinterest Board or Display your Pinterest Profile.
* [Advaced Version Link](http://wordpress.techgasp.com/pinterest-master/)

= Insert Widget in Pages or Posts =
* ADVANCED Version constains the shortcode framework
* Insert the code into the page or post. [pinterestmaster id=x]
* "x" stands for your sidebar number, ie sidebar-1, final code would be:
* [pinterestmaster id=sidebar-1]
* [Advaced Version Link](http://wordpress.techgasp.com/pinterest-master/)
